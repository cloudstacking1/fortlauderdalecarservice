Frequently Asked Questions

What areas does your transportation service cover?

Our transportation service covers all of Fort Lauderdale, Miami, Palm Beach, and the entire South Florida region. We provide airport transfers to and from Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport, Miami International Airport, and Palm Beach International Airport, as well as transportation to Port Everglades and other major destinations throughout the area.

How far in advance should I book my limousine service?

For optimal availability, we recommend booking at least 48-72 hours in advance, especially for special events like weddings, proms, or large group transportation. However, we also accommodate last-minute bookings when possible. For peak seasons and popular dates, booking several weeks ahead ensures you get your preferred vehicle.

What types of vehicles are available in your fleet?

Our comprehensive fleet includes luxury sedans, SUV limousines, stretch limos, vans for group transportation, and party buses. Each vehicle is equipped with premium amenities such as leather seating, climate control, entertainment systems, and complimentary refreshments. We can accommodate groups ranging from individuals to large parties of 20+ passengers.

Do you monitor flights for airport transportation?

Yes, absolutely. For all airport pickups, we monitor your flight status in real-time to adjust pickup times based on any delays or early arrivals. This ensures your chauffeur is ready and waiting when you land, eliminating stress and wait times. We also provide complimentary meet-and-greet service inside the terminal for added convenience.

What is your cancellation and refund policy?

We offer flexible cancellation terms depending on the service booked. For standard reservations, cancellations made 24 hours or more in advance receive a full refund. Special event bookings like weddings may have different terms due to advance vehicle reservations. We always work with our clients to find fair solutions. Please contact us at 954-666-1067 to discuss specific circumstances.
